post apocalyptic hardcore survival horror game

„…there's only a few survivors and we are one of them”

Overview

The No More Light is a hardcore survivor horror game whereas a gamer you will gonna find yourself in a huge destroyed world that is under-occupied by aliens. Your main goal is to save humanity from the total extinction, but first YOU must be reached the next day... It s also a serious challenge. Fast-changing weather, alien bacteria, low ammo, food, and medkit, as well as stony enemies, will cause you a lot of trouble at the gameplay. It is up to you what strategy you follow, but be careful to make the right choice, because in this world there is no second chance...

ATMOSPHERE

There are many dangers involved in this enormous open world. Because of the alien bacteria, that are deadly to all living things on earth recommended to wear protective equipment. If you choose to avoid them you still need to be careful! In the destroyed towns and woods plenty of aliens are waiting to catch you... Because of these, and many other things the No More Light has got a very depressing, post-apocalyptic atmosphere, which will captivate you in the first moment.

ENVIRONMENT

We are in the countryside of Colorado State in the United States.

There are over 100 exclusive detailed places like military bases, bunkers, caves, cities, farms, marshes, factories, wreck sites waiting for you to explore them. But be careful! In this fast-changing darkness world only one thing sure: you are in danger! The only question is: Are you ready to fight and survive?

ACTION

Only thing matters The Fight!

You must be fight against the brutal aliens who are constantly chasing you
You must be fight against the weather what changes in a seconds
You must be fight for the food. There are only a few and others want it as well.
You must be fight against the low ammo. There is a little of it and run out quickly in a battle.
You must be fight against your condition. There s not enough medicine so better if you don t hurt yourself. But you will... And finally you must be fight against you to don t give up...

THE INVADERS

They are travel in the galaxy thousands of years, and invade every planet and use them for themselves, after that they move on. This time the Earth and humanity is the next. Their technology is more advanced than the humans, their power is a multiple of the human, and they have thousands of years of experience in the galactic warfare. Because they live FOR war because they live FROM war.

GREYS

The situation is very hopeless and futureless, you don't need to fight alone against the Invaders! The Greys will help you with this. They have been protecting humanity invisibly for thousands of years, and now they are entering the scene because with the occupation of our planet, theirs began to be in danger as well. They will help you, and if they need they will fight with you, but not instead of you!
