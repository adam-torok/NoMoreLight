"CAN YOU SURVIVE AN ALIEN INVASION"

OVERVIEW

"...Only a few survive, will you?"

No More Light is a unique breed of hardcore survival horror game, where you will find yourself in a huge destroyed world, that is occupied by aliens. Your main goal is to survive by ANY means necessary. The whole world and almost all of it's inhabitants are against you. Dangerous weather system, alien bacteria, spacial anomalies, shortage of food, ammunition and medicine means that you will have to fight for your survival every waking moment. Every wrong decision, missed shot or enemy you make along the way could mean that tomorrow you will see No More Light.....

The Greys

Your future is hopeless...

...but you are not alone in your struggles. The Greys have been with humanity for centuries, protecting them from grave dangers but now that the survival of the entire species is at stakes they came out of their hiding. They will help you, with resources and will even fight alongside you but everything comes at a cost....

THE INVADERS

They are traveling the galaxy for thousands of years,invading every planet to take them for themselves, and move on.

Having destroyed their own planet in a desperate search for resources they have been roaming the galaxy in search for more. Earth is nothing for them just the next pit stop in their never-ending quest for survival..

ABOUT THE GAME

ATMOSPHERE

There are many dangers residing in this enormous open world. Every corner of the world has it's own challenges that could kill the player at any moment. Spacial anomalies, alien bacteria, radiation, alien invaders roaming around. You may choose to avoid one but you cannot avoid them all. Because of the constant pressure and fight for survival No More Light has a depressing, postapocalyptic atmosphere like no other.

ENVIRONMENT

We are in the countryside of Colorado U.S.A. There are over 100 exclusive detailed places like military bases, bunkers, caves, cities, farms, marshes, factories, wreck sites waiting for you to explore them. But be careful! In this fast-changing dark world there's only one thing that's for granted: You are in danger! The only question is: Are you prepared?

GAMEPLAY

The only thing that's constant is the struggle! You must fight against the aliens who are always close-by, the weather that's always changing and always dangerous, for the few resources that are left in this world but you are not the only one fighting. You will run across others who are fighting for the same scarce resources as you but violence is not always the best option. Every spent bullet, every time you get hurt, every wrong decision could and WILL mean your death.
